Eco Eats Restaurant

Come check out our amazing Eco Eats restaurant, located in Temple Gardens at the heart of Bristol. We are proud to offer our amazing, tasty and healthy food on a pay as you feel basis so that you can enjoy our fabulous restaurant and pay only what you can. We offer a rotating list of seasonal specials that add a dynamic flavour to our restaurant, as well as our traditional menu featuring outstanding healthy meals.
