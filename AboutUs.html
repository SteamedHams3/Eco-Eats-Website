About Us

Here at Eco Eats we are passionate about solving the issue of food waste and helping the environment, whilst providing a boost for our local community. That is why we started this company, and why we made the decision to make our services such as our amazing Eco Eats Restaurant, and our phenomenal subscription food boxes, available on a pay as you feel basis. This not only helps protect the environment, but encourages members of our community to eat healthily and sustainably, regarless of financial situation.

Eco Eats Aims and Objectives

At Eco Eats we are focussed on helping the environment and solving the problem of food waste. We aim to achieve this through providing delicious, nourishing and healthy food on a pay as you feel basis both at our amazing Eco Eats restaurant as well as our healthy fruit, veg and other food items available in our food boxes. We want to help our community thrive and protect our planet whilst providing great food for you!

Eco Eats Products and Services

We are proud to offer a great range of environmentally friendly products and services, such as our outstanding Eco Eats resaurant, that provides a great menu full of tasty and healthy meals that are good for the environment, and our food box service that contains healthy and tasty fruit, veg and much more. All of these are available on a pay as you feel basis as we want to empower members of our commmunity to engage with the food waste issue, helping the environment and bringing our community closer!
