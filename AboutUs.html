About Us

Here at Eco Eats we are passionate about solving the issue of food waste and helping the environment, whilst providing a boost for our local community. That is why we started this company, and why we made the decision to make our services such as our amazing Eco Eats Restaurant, and our phenomenal subscription food boxes, available on a pay as you feel basis. This not only helps protect the environment, but encourages members of our community to eat healthily and sustainably, regarless of financial situation.

Eco Eats Pay Aims and Objectives

At Eco Eats we pride ourselves on providing great tasting, sustainable food, that is both good for environment and helps our community thrive. We are proud to offer the outstanding pay as you feel service, where customers can pay only what they can afford, giving our whole community the chance to eat out and have nice food, whilst helping the environment.
