Eco Eats Pay as you feel Restaurant

At Eco Eats we pride ourselves on providing great tasting, sustainable food, that is both good for environment and helps our community thrive. We are proud to offer the outstanding pay as you feel service, where customers can pay only what they can afford, giving our whole community the chance to eat out and have nice food, whilst helping the environment.

Eco Eats Community food boxes

At Eco Eats we are very passionate about helping our community, and so we decided to launch our fresh fruit, veg and other cupbard goods subscription boxes, also on a pay as you feel basis. This enables members of our community to recieve fresh ingredients and make exciting and vibrant meals and only pay what they can.
