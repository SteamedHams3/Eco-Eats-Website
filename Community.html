Community

At Eco Eats, we are passionate about empowering our community and bringing everyone together! We love providing tasty, sustanaible and eco friendly food, that helps the problem of food waste both through our Eco Eats restaurant and our community food boes. All of our food is available on a pay as you feel basis, so all members of our community can enjoy great tasting food and help solve the problem of food waste!

Our Sustanaible Community Food Boxes

At Eco Eats we pride ourselves on providing great tasting, sustainable food, that is both good for environment and helps our community thrive. We are proud to offer the outstanding pay as you feel service, where customers can pay only what they can afford, giving our whole community the chance to eat out and have nice food, whilst helping the environment.
