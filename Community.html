Community

At Eco Eats, we are passionate about empowering our community and bringing everyone together! We love providing tasty, sustanaible and eco friendly food, that helps the problem of food waste both through our Eco Eats restaurant and our community food boes. All of our food is available on a pay as you feel basis, so all members of our community can enjoy great tasting food and help solve the problem of food waste!

Our Sustanaible Community Food Boxes

At Eco Eats, we strive to provide, great tasting and healthy food that is environmentally friendly and also solves the problem of food waste in our community. We are proud to offer food boxes featuring fresh fruit and veg, and much more, with all of our food being environmentally friendly and sustainable. Our food boxes are available on a pay as you feel basis, which enables all members of our community to be able to eat healthily and sustanaibly. Let's tackle food waste together!

Our Environmental Community Education

At Eco Eats, we love helping our community thrive! All of our food products are also available on a pay as you feel basis so that all members of our community can enjoy great tasing, sustanaible food!
